Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026

1. Scope

This tool is a personal automation script used only by its owner (the operator of the TikTok account @lamsuahatcungto) to post cooking videos to the owner's own social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok). It is not distributed to or used by any third party.

2. Data Collected

The tool only accesses:

  • Video files stored locally on the owner's computer.
  • OAuth tokens issued by Facebook, Google, and TikTok for the owner's own accounts.

No data is collected from any other user.

3. Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third party. OAuth tokens are stored locally on the owner's machine and are only used to call the official platform APIs to post the owner's own videos.

4. Data Retention

Tokens are kept on the owner's machine until manually revoked. Posted videos remain on the social platforms per each platform's own policy.
